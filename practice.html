GUIDELINES

Start your engines and join the Rampage Rally, our next vehicle-based 3D Community Challenge. We challenge you to use the provided template as a blueprint to drive your own unique creation over the next 30 days.

Your art will be judged on idea/story, lighting & render quality, composition, and animation. Follow the rules below to guarantee your place in the "All Renders" Community Montage, or push for a spot in the curated Top 100 Montage.

Good luck, have fun and we'll see you at the finish line!

RULES

  • 01 Replace the Vehicle Proxy with your own vehicle.
  • 02 Jump the provided gap with your vehicle.
  • 03 Maintain the sense of speed and direction.
  • 04 Use the provided camera (more info).
  • 05 One submission per artist, one artist per submission.
  • 06 You must follow the technical specifications (Name rule, resolution, frame count, file type, codex, file size)
  • 07 No generative AI is allowed. (more info).
  • 08 Submit by Monday 1 September 2025 at 12:29 GMT+5:30 (+ 2 weeks, 6 days)
