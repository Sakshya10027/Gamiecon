ABOUT GAMIECON

Gamiecon is a competitive platform where student developers showcase original game concepts through working prototypes and Game Design Documents (GDDs). The most promising projects will be selected by an expert jury and receive hands-on mentorship to help bring their games to life—from development to publishing and marketing.

IMPORTANT DETAILS

1. Teams of 1-5 members will register.

2. Submissions must include a detailed Game Design Document (GDD) and if possible a working game prototype as well.

3. The GDD should outline the game's core mechanics, story, art style, target audience, and monetization strategy.

4. Each team will have a dedicated time slot for their presentation, followed by a Q&A session with the judges.

5. An expert jury, comprising professionals from the gaming industry, will evaluate all submitted projects based on originality, technical execution, and market viability.

6. Submission Deadline: September 17, 2025

7. Event Date: September 18, 2025

8. Venue: Gaming Lab (LC005), VIT Bhopal

FAQ

You've got questions, we've got answers!

If you're still unsure about anything or have further questions, you can always ask on our Discord.

Q: What is Gamiecon?
Gamiecon is a competitive platform for student developers to showcase original game concepts through working prototypes and Game Design Documents (GDDs).
Q: Who can participate?
Gamiecon is open to all students, from all majors and skill levels. You can participate as an individual or on a team.
Q: What kind of projects are you looking for?
We're looking for original, innovative game concepts of any genre for any platform, including PC, console, mobile, and VR/AR.
Q: How are projects judged?
An expert jury of industry professionals will judge projects on originality, technical feasibility, game design, and GDD quality.
Q: What are the prizes?
The best projects will receive hands-on mentorship, as well as publishing and marketing support to help bring their games to life.
Q: Can I present the game I'm already working on?
Yes, you can present a game you're already working on. .
Q: I'm a beginner. Can I still participate?
Yes! Gamiecon is an excellent opportunity to learn, collaborate, and grow as a developer. .
